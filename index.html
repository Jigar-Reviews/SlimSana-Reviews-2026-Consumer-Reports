🍵
Green Tea Extract (EGCG)
Thermogenic Activator

Rich in epigallocatechin gallate, green tea extract promotes thermogenesis by increasing norepinephrine activity in fat cells. Research suggests consistent supplementation supports a modest but measurable increase in resting metabolic rate. It also provides antioxidant support that helps reduce oxidative stress associated with caloric restriction.

⚖️
Chromium Picolinate
Blood Sugar Regulator

Chromium is a trace mineral that enhances insulin receptor sensitivity, allowing cells to take up glucose more efficiently. Clinical evidence links chromium supplementation to reduced carbohydrate cravings and lower fasting blood sugar variability. This makes it particularly relevant for individuals whose weight gain is driven by reactive eating after blood sugar dips.

🌿
Berberine HCl
AMPK Metabolic Switch

Berberine activates AMP-activated protein kinase, the enzyme that governs cellular energy balance. Studies show it supports improved glucose metabolism, reduced lipid accumulation in adipose tissue, and better insulin sensitivity. Multiple trials indicate meaningful BMI reductions over 8 to 12 weeks, positioning berberine as one of the most researched non-prescription metabolic compounds.

🍎
Apple Cider Vinegar
Satiety and Digestion

Acetic acid in apple cider vinegar slows gastric emptying, increasing the duration of post-meal satiety signals. Research suggests it also supports healthy gut microbiome diversity and eases bloating, both of which contribute to more comfortable adherence to reduced-calorie eating. Users in many slim sachet and slimsip reviews-style comparisons rate this ingredient highly for digestive comfort.

🫘
White Kidney Bean Extract
Carb Absorption Blocker

White kidney bean contains phaseolamin, an alpha-amylase inhibitor that partially blocks the digestion of dietary starches. By reducing the caloric impact of carbohydrate-heavy meals, it supports a lower net caloric intake without requiring strict dietary avoidance. Clinical evidence indicates it is particularly effective when consumed around meals with significant starch content.

🌱
Ashwagandha (KSM-66)
Adaptogen / Stress Metabolism

Ashwagandha is classified as an adaptogen, helping the body manage cortisol, the stress hormone strongly linked to abdominal fat accumulation. Research suggests that lowering chronic cortisol levels through adaptogen supplementation supports healthier body composition, particularly around the midsection. It also contributes to improved sleep quality, which is increasingly recognized as a weight management variable.

🌺
Rhodiola Rosea
Energy and Recovery

Rhodiola is an adaptogenic herb shown to reduce physical and mental fatigue, supporting more consistent daily activity levels. Studies indicate it can improve exercise endurance and reduce perceived exertion, meaning supplemented individuals are more likely to sustain physical activity routines. This indirect effect on energy expenditure complements the direct thermogenic ingredients in the formula.

🧠
Alpha-GPC
Cognitive Focus Support

Alpha-GPC is a choline compound that supports acetylcholine synthesis in the brain, contributing to improved focus, memory, and mental clarity. Its inclusion in a weight management formula is notable because cognitive fatigue and decision-making impairment are significant contributors to dietary inconsistency. Research indicates Alpha-GPC also supports growth hormone secretion, which has mild body composition implications.
